Chocolate Protein Pudding

Rich and Sweet

Chocolate protein pudding is the perfect afternoon snack or healthy dessert!

Ingredients:

  • 1 serving chocolate protein powder
  • 2 tbsp cacao powder
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 tbsp nut butter

Steps:

  1. Combine the protein powder, cacao powder and milk in a bowl. Stir well until most of the lumps are fully removed. Start with 3/4 cup of dairy free milk and add a little at a time so it doesn't get too runny.
  2. Once the lumps are gone, add the creamy almond butter or melted coconut butter and stir well until you have a pudding consistency.
  3. Refrigerate up to 8 hours or serve immediately.
