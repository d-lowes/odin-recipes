Powerhouse Protein Parfaits

Healthy and Hearty

Parfaits have marvelous taste and texture, but many are loaded with sugar and leave you peckish for more. Hereâ€™s my protein-inspired option with yogurt, fruit and nuts.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 2 teaspoons grated orange zest
  • 2 cups Kashi Go Lean Crunch cereal
  • 2 cups orange segments
  • 2 cups fresh raspberries
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds, toasted

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, honey and orange zest until blended.
  2. Layer half the yogurt mixture, cereal, orange segments and raspberries among four parfait glasses.
  3. Repeat layers; sprinkle with almonds.
  4. Serve immediately.
